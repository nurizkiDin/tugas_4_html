Halaman 3 - MOTO GP

Dovizioso: The Attendance King

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the grid behind Valention Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last tow years has been on paper - Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
